This is an ongoing, peer-centered support group for adults grieving the loss of a significant adult including sibling, friend or other connected adult.

Pre-registration is required for those attending the group for the first time.

There is no fee.

Time: 6-7:30 PM

For more information, call 502-456-5451, or submit an online contact form: https://hosparushealth.org/contact-us/
